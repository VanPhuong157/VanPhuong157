TEA

Tea is the second most popular beverage in the world. It is made by soaking the leaves, buds, or branches of the tea plant in boiling water for a few minutes to several hours. Tea leaves can be oxidized, roasted, dried, or mixed with other herbs such as flowers, spices, or other fruits before being boiled in water.
